About SSOL

From humble beginnings as used oil traders to becoming a trusted name in integrated hazardous waste management — this is the story of Shiv Shakti Oil & Lubricants.

Shiv Shakti Oil & Lubricants (SSOL) was built on a simple idea: nothing should go to waste if it can be recovered, recycled, or repurposed responsibly. Based in Bhiwadi, Rajasthan, what began as a small used-oil trading operation has grown into a fully integrated hazardous waste management company — combining preprocessing, recycling, incineration, and logistics under one roof, backed by ISO-certified quality, environmental, and safety standards.
