Building Sustainable Solutions Since 2003

Our Journey

Every enduring organization begins with a belief. Ours began with the conviction that what many considered waste still held value. In 2003, Shri Bikram Dhir, together with his sons Mr. Sandeep Dhir and Mr. Sunny Dhir, laid the foundation of Shiv Shakti Oil & Lubricants with a simple yet powerful vision—to recover valuable resources through responsible recycling while contributing towards environmental conservation. The company began as a trusted trader of used lubricating oil. But from the very beginning, the objective extended beyond trading. The vision was to establish a modern re-refining facility capable of transforming used oil into high-quality base oil, giving valuable resources a second life through sustainable technology. That vision became the foundation of everything we have built today.
